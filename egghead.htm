Description

Who Wants To Be An Egghead is a recreation of the US clock format version of Who Wants to Be a Millionaire tv show. Will you be able to show your friends you are a true egghead?

Rules:
You get questions with four possible answers. Answer 15 questions correctly and you win. Just to make your job easier, you will get 3 life lines.

Your life lines are the following ones:
Tick-Tock: Call your wise friend who knows the answer for everything. Be quick, you will only get 30 seconds to chit-chat.
Survey: Ask the world for their opinion. Somebody might know the correct answer...
Double Check: Are you hesitating between 2 answers? Choose both!

Notes:
-This version is only the alpha. It contains only 1 question. Feel free to fool around with the buttons.
-The public version does not contain any music nor sound effects due to copyright issues.
